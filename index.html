Zoo Grand Opening!

Today was Zootopia Zoo's grand opening. When I was there it was hot and sunny, there a lots of animals like zebras, girrafes, monkeys, Tigers, And their main attraction, The Lion! The animals that looked well fed which is good for animal enthusiast. There are diffrent regions of the zoo. When you enter the zoo, you are in the Artic section with the Polar bears, penguins, Yaks, etc. Then there is the Safari, then the Mesa, Then desert, then rainforest, then forest, and finally the aquatic section. "We will be adding new species every year". Says the owner of the Zootopia zoo.

Top 5 Animal Attractions of the Zootopia Zoo:

  1. Lion
  2. Tiger
  3. Monkey
  4. Polar Bear
  5. Penguin
